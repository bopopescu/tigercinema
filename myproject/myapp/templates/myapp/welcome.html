TigerCinema

Hi there! TigerCinema is an online video sharing platform for Princeton students who make movies and wish to share their work with their peers. It is created by Haochen Li, Iden Kalemaj, and Yuyan Zhao for COS 333 project.

Please note that while you are very welcome to stream these movies on our site, downloading and sharing with other people without the permission of the author is strictly prohibited.

Now, let's get started!
